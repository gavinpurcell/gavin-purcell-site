# gavinpurcell.com Redesign — Inbound-Focused

## Goal

Reposition gavinpurcell.com from personal brand showcase to a site that generates inbound for:
1. Fractional Chief Content Officer / consulting engagements (primary)
2. Full-time leadership roles at studios and AI companies (implied, not stated)

## Target Visitors

- Recruiters/execs who just heard Gavin's name and are googling him
- Potential consulting clients (AI companies needing creative leadership, studios scaling AI content, brands building media arms)
- People referred by word-of-mouth who need to see credibility fast

## Current Structure (being replaced)

1. Hero (AI image toy)
2. About (bio + highlight cards)
3. AI For Humans (podcast showcase)
4. AndThen (startup showcase)
5. Featured Blog Post
6. Consulting (4 generic cards + FAQ + contact)

## New Structure

### 1. Hero
- Strong positioning headline: the "media + AI" rare combo
- Subhead that frames consulting as the primary offer
- Professional photo (static, no gimmick)
- Two CTAs: "See My Work" / "Work With Me" (anchor links)
- Keep the neo-brutalist design language

### 2. Credibility Bar
- Horizontal logo strip showing where Gavin has worked/been featured
- Logos: The Tonight Show, NBC, Vox Media, G4, a16z, Fast Company
- Grayscale or muted treatment, compact row
- Text fallback if logos are hard to source

### 3. The Offer — Fractional CCO
- NEW section, the money section
- Headline: something like "Your Fractional Chief Content Officer"
- 2-3 paragraphs: what fractional CCO means, who it's for, what you get
- Target clients spelled out: AI companies needing creative leadership, studios scaling AI workflows, brands building media operations
- This is NOT a generic consulting pitch — it's a specific, named role offer

### 4. Track Record
- Reworked from current About section
- Results-oriented, not resume-oriented
- Key highlights as punchy stat/achievement cards:
  - Tonight Show: ran 60-person team, 5 nights/week, 10M+ viewers
  - Vox/G4: built digital content orgs
  - AI For Humans: 35K+ YouTube subs, top AI podcast
  - AndThen: a16z-backed AI startup founder
- Brief narrative tying it together (3-4 sentences max)

### 5. Current Projects
- AI For Humans + AndThen collapsed into one compact section
- These are credentials showing he's active in the space
- Small embed or thumbnail + short description for each
- NOT the centerpiece of the page

### 6. Services
- Tighter than current. Three cards max:
  - **Fractional CCO / Content Leadership** — embed with your team, build the org
  - **AI Strategy & Implementation** — practical AI integration for content teams
  - **Speaking & Workshops** — keynotes, team trainings, exec workshops
- Drop "Digital Transformation" (too vague) and "Media & Content Strategy" (redundant with CCO)

### 7. Contact CTA
- Prominent section with clear email CTA
- "Let's talk" framing, not "ready to start a conversation?"
- Direct mailto link, large and obvious
- Optional: brief "What to expect" — "I'll reply within 48 hours, we'll set up a 30-minute call"

### Removed
- AI image transformer toy (fun but wrong signal)
- FAQ section (third-person SEO filler, not needed)
- Featured blog post section (can add back later if blog becomes active)

## Design Notes
- Keep existing neo-brutalist aesthetic
- Existing CSS files can be reused/adapted
- Build on a preview branch for review before deploying
- Source logos as SVGs where possible, text fallback where not
